Biography Artist statement CV

I create surreal images using a camera. In my art, I explore the psychology of interactions between people and their environment. For each photograph, I develop a scenario and manually create the decorations, which can include giant lobster claws, face masks, flowers with eyes, etc. Depending on the concept, I might sculpt, embroider, paint, or apply makeup. In my work, I do not use artificial intelligence or computer graphics. I only perform color correction on the photos using graphic editors.

Since emotions are invisual, I enjoy expressing them visually. I believe that emotions are mostly expressed through the face, which is why I often use the trick of hiding the character's face and conveying emotions through other objects or body parts. I invite the viewer into a world where reality and fantasy blend, and surreal scenes serve as a bridge between the inner world of emotions and external reality.
